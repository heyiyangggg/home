Urban & Grassroots

How cities are
made — and
by whom.

Urban development is never just technocratic planning. It is shaped by political institutions, grassroots movements, and the social histories embedded in place. I examine how these forces interact in contexts from Singapore's planned towns to caste and social inequality in global cities.

Urban Grassroots in the Making of Jurong Town — article in progress with A/P Daniel P. S. Goh (NUS)
Global Caste Lab — researcher & digital manager with Asst Prof Suraj Milind Yengde (University of Pennsylvania)
Urban × Energy — Climate Justice

Who pays for
the energy
transition?

Urban space and energy systems are deeply entangled. Climate transitions reshape neighbourhoods, school districts, and regional economies — often concentrating new risks on communities already underserved. I study both the politics of this unevenness and the institutional designs that could change it.

Climate Justice in K–12 School District Sustainability Planning — article in progress with Asst Prof Akira Drake Rodriguez (University of Pennsylvania)
Just and Inclusive Energy Transitions in South and Southeast Asia — article in progress with Prof Petra Tschakert (NUS)
Urban × AI — Infrastructure & Place

AI infrastructure
is urban
infrastructure.

Data centres, cloud systems, and AI pipelines are physical — they consume land, water, and power, and they are governed by policy choices that have real spatial consequences. I examine how US data centre policy shapes cities and communities.

US Data Center Policy — article in progress with Dr Vasundhara Kaul, Simon An, Asst Prof Daniel S. Schiff (Purdue)
AI, Education & the Future of Work

How AI is
reshaping who
works, and how.

AI is restructuring labour markets, classrooms, and professional life — but its effects aren't neutral. I study how workers navigate precarity, how AI will reshape workforces, and how educational institutions can be redesigned to serve learners on their own terms.

"It's Messy…But I Feel Balanced" — published, CHI '26, with Daniel Campos Muñiz, Tse Pei Ng, Asst Prof Janghee Cho, A/P Jung-Joo Lee, Ker Wey Aw
Predicting the Impacts of AI on Workers: A Systematic Literature Review — in progress with Asst Prof Luísa Nazareno (VCU), Asst Prof Daniel Schiff (Purdue), Asst Prof Zeewan Lee (LKY SPP)
A Socio-Ethical Research Agenda for AI and the Future of Work — book chapter in progress with Luísa Nazareno, Vasundhara Kaul et al.
Intentional Design: GenAI Integration Through the Human-AI Matrix — conference paper, HECS 2025, with Dr Verily Tan, Dr Mei Hui Liu, Dr Chun Liang Terrence Tan, A/P Yuen Jien Soo (NUS)
